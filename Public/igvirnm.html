Overview

The mission behind
the campaign

AIESEC in SLIIT’s iGV (Incoming Global Volunteer) programme connects Sri Lankan youth with international volunteering experiences that create real-world social impact. As part of the IR & M (International Relations and Marketing) team, I was responsible for shaping the visual identity of our attraction campaigns. The goal was to create content that could stand out on social media while speaking directly to purpose-driven students.

The challenge was to balance AIESEC’s global brand guidelines with a tone that felt local, personal, and compelling enough to drive sign-ups during a highly competitive recruitment period.

Process
How this came together
01
Research & Brief

Studied AIESEC’s global brand guidelines and reviewed past campaigns from other local committees. Defined a tone that was youthful, purposeful, and bold, while identifying the most effective platforms and formats to maximise reach.

02
Concept & Mood

Developed a visual direction centred on contrast and urgency, using vibrant accent colours against dark backgrounds and a strong typographic hierarchy to guide attention toward the call to action. Created mood boards before moving into design.

03
Design & Execution

Built a modular system of templates in Figma to ensure speed and consistency across the team. Each post followed a structured grid, allowing flexibility in content while maintaining a cohesive visual identity throughout the campaign.

04
Review & Launch

Refined designs based on feedback from the iGV IR & M VP and AIESEC in Sri Lanka’s national standards. Final assets were approved and published across Global WhatsApp groups and Instagram during the peak attraction period.

Outcomes
What it achieved
◈
Increased Reach
Campaign posts consistently outperformed organic benchmarks, expanding visibility for AIESEC in SLIIT’s iGV programme across campus.
◈
Template System
Developed a reusable design framework that enabled the team to create future campaign content efficiently while maintaining brand consistency.
◈
Cross-Platform Execution
Assets were deployed across WhatsApp and Instagram with platform-specific adaptations, ensuring a consistent visual identity across all touchpoints.
◈
Attraction Goal Met
Contributed to achieving the iGV IR & M team’s attraction target for the cycle, connecting more students to international volunteering opportunities.
