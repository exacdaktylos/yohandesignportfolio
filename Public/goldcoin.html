Overview

The idea behind
the project

This project involved designing posters and invitation materials for a large-scale corporate award ceremony hosted by a leading organization in the apparel industry. The objective was to create visuals that reflected the prestige of the event while communicating key information clearly and effectively.

The challenge was to balance professionalism with visual appeal, ensuring that every asset aligned with corporate standards while still feeling engaging. The designs needed to maintain a high level of clarity, hierarchy, and consistency across both print and digital formats.

Process
How this came together
01
Research & Brief

Reviewed the event requirements and corporate branding expectations to understand the tone and level of formality required.

02
Concept Development

Explored clean and structured design directions that emphasized hierarchy, readability, and a sense of prestige suitable for an award ceremony.

03
Design & Execution

Developed posters and invitation materials with a focus on typography, spacing, and layout to ensure clarity across both print and digital formats.

04
Refinement & Delivery

Refined designs based on feedback, ensuring alignment with expectations before finalizing assets for distribution.
