Overview

The idea behind
the application

The National Leadership Development Seminar (NLDS) is a flagship AIESEC experience focused on developing leadership among members across the country. As part of my application for the Organising Committee, I created a series of visual assets to communicate ideas, showcase creativity, and demonstrate my approach to campaign design.

The challenge was to present concepts in a way that was both visually engaging and strategically clear, while aligning with AIESEC’s brand guidelines. The content needed to stand out, communicate intent effectively, and reflect my ability to contribute to a high-impact national event.

Process
How this came together
01
Research & Brief

Studied AIESEC’s brand guidelines and reviewed past national event content to understand expectations. Defined a tone that was bold, clear, and aligned with leadership-focused messaging.

02
Concept & Mood

Explored visual directions that reflected energy and impact, using strong typography and contrast to emphasise key messages. Developed initial concepts before moving into design.

03
Design & Execution

Created a series of cohesive assets that showcased both creativity and structure. Focused on clarity, layout balance, and visual consistency across all pieces.

04
Review & Launch

Iterated on designs to improve clarity, alignment, and overall presentation, ensuring each asset effectively communicated its intended message.
