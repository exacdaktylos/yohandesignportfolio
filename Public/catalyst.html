Overview

The idea behind
the campaign

Catalyst 5.0 is AIESEC in SLIIT’s flagship Leadership Development Seminar, designed to inspire and develop future leaders. As Organising Committee Vice President for Marketing, I was responsible for leading the overall marketing direction and execution of the event.

The campaign was built around an Avatar: The Last Airbender-inspired theme, using familiar elements and storytelling to create a strong emotional connection with the audience. The challenge was to translate this concept into a cohesive visual identity while maintaining alignment with AIESEC’s brand guidelines and ensuring the campaign remained engaging, relevant, and effective in driving participation.

Process
How this came together
01
Research & Brief

Analyzed past event campaigns and aligned with AIESEC’s brand guidelines. Defined a direction that combined leadership messaging with a strong thematic identity.

02
Concept & Theme Development

Built the campaign around an Avatar-inspired concept, translating elements of storytelling, character, and progression into a visual and narrative direction that resonated with the audience.

03
Design & Execution

Led the creation of campaign assets, ensuring consistency across all platforms. Focused on strong typography, visual hierarchy, and thematic elements to maintain a cohesive identity.

04
Review & Launch

Refined assets based on team feedback and rolled out the campaign across Instagram, LinkedIn, and other digital platforms during the event promotion phase.
