Overview

The idea behind
the work

This work focused on creating merchandise for AIESEC in SLIIT, alongside a selection of personal apparel designs. The goal was to translate brand identity and ideas into wearable formats that felt both visually appealing and meaningful to the audience.

The challenge was to design graphics that worked effectively on fabric, balancing creativity with practicality. Each piece needed to be visually strong, adaptable across different apparel types, and aligned with the intended audience, whether for events, members, or personal expression.

Process
How this came together
01
Research & Inspiration

Explored existing merchandise trends, brand guidelines, and visual references to understand what resonates in apparel design.

02
Concept Development

Developed design concepts that balanced creativity with wearability, focusing on bold visuals, typography, and placement.

03
Design & Execution

Created graphics tailored for apparel, ensuring scalability, print clarity, and adaptability across different formats such as t-shirts and jackets.

04
Refinement & Delivery

Iterated on designs to improve composition, readability, and overall visual impact, preparing final outputs for production.
