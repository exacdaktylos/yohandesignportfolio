How I work
The journey behind
every project
I follow a learner-centred design process — every decision traces back to a real person trying to learn something real.
01
Discover
Understanding the learner — who they are, what they know, and what needs to change.
02
Design
Storyboards, wireframes, and objectives mapped before any tool is opened. Structure first.
03
Build
Development in Articulate, Rise, or Figma — accessibility and LMS compatibility from day one.
04
Evaluate
Pilot testing, learner feedback, and iteration. Measuring actual knowledge transfer.

Let's build something learners actually remember
